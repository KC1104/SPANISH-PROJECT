Alhambra

The Alhambra is a magnificent palace and fortress complex in Granada, renowned for its Islamic architecture and stunning gardens.

Sagrada Familia

Designed by Antoni Gaudí, the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona is a breathtaking example of modernist architecture.

Royal Palace of Madrid

The Royal Palace of Madrid is the official residence of the Spanish Royal Family, showcasing opulent interiors and historic artifacts.

Park Güell

Park Güell, another masterpiece by Gaudí, features colorful mosaics, whimsical architecture, and panoramic views of Barcelona.

Seville Cathedral

One of the largest Gothic cathedrals in the world, the Seville Cathedral is home to the tomb of Christopher Columbus.

Cathedral of Barcelona

The Cathedral of Barcelona is a striking example of Catalan Gothic architecture, with its intricate façade and peaceful cloister.
