Spanish Culture

Spain’s culture is a vivid mix of traditions and modern life. From the passionate Flamenco dance to festivals like La Tomatina, Spain’s cultural diversity is reflected in its art, architecture, and lifestyle.

Top Attractions in Spain

Sagrada Familia

The stunning basilica in Barcelona designed by Gaudi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Alhambra

An exquisite palace and fortress complex located in Granada, a symbol of Moorish architecture.

Plaza Mayor

A grand square in the heart of Madrid, surrounded by historical buildings and cafes.
